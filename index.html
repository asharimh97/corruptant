Welcome to Corruptant

Corruptant is a web project which made by SIS Rangers (Ashari, Faldy, Bastian, Natsir) to participate in Hackathon Merdeka 3.0. We made it as a reporter website about corruption surrounding people. People Will be free to report to us about what corruption has been happened in their society, and we will communicate it to government (far development).
